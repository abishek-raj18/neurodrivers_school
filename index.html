Our Approach to Care

Every child at SEED receives a comprehensive assessment and an individualized therapy plan. Our therapists work in close collaboration with educators and families to ensure consistent, effective support.

📊 Progress Monitoring

We conduct Parent-Teacher Meetings every 3 months to review student progress, reassess goals, and adapt therapy plans. Data-driven decision making ensures every intervention is effective.

🌟 Once a month, parents are welcome to attend classes and observe firsthand how their children adapt to the classroom environment — building transparency and trust.

🤝 Family-Centered Model

We believe parents are essential partners. Our parental counselling sessions empower families with the knowledge and tools to support their child's growth at home.

🏫 About Our Classroom

A nurturing, structured, and inclusive class focusing on communication, behaviour, academics, motor skills, sensory needs, and daily living skills. Every child learns with love, support, and specialized guidance.

A Passion for
Every Child's Potential

Our founder brings over 10 years of dedicated experience in the field of Neurodiversity, working with children across a wide range of developmental conditions including Autism (ASD), ADHD, Cerebral Palsy, and Intellectual Disabilities.

With a strong background in special education, physiotherapy, occupational therapy, and child development, she has been instrumental in creating inclusive environments that promote learning, independence, and emotional growth.

Her vision is to provide every child — regardless of ability — with the opportunity to learn through play-based methods, therapy support, and personalized care. With a deep passion for empowering neurodiverse children and guiding their families, she continues to advocate for awareness, inclusion, and early intervention.

She has also completed specialized intensive training for working with children with autism, bringing cutting-edge, evidence-based practices to Seed Neurodiverse.

"Every child is capable of growth and joy when given the right support, love, and opportunity. At SEED, we believe in the extraordinary potential within every neurodiverse child."
