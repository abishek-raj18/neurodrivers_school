Signs of Autism in Children — A Parent's Guide

Published: May 2026 • By Seed Neurodiverse, Coimbatore • 6 min read

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a developmental condition that affects how a child communicates, interacts, and experiences the world. As a leading autism therapy center in Coimbatore, Seed Neurodiverse helps parents recognize early signs and take timely action.

Early Signs of Autism in Children

1. Social Communication Differences

  • Limited or no eye contact during interactions
  • Delayed speech or language development (not speaking by 16 months)
  • Difficulty understanding or using gestures (pointing, waving)

2. Behavioral Patterns

  • Repetitive movements such as hand-flapping, rocking, or spinning
  • Strong preference for routines and distress at changes
  • Intense focus on specific objects or topics

💡 Important: Having one or two of these signs does not mean your child has autism. However, if you notice several of these patterns consistently, we recommend consulting a specialist.

How Early Intervention Helps

Research consistently shows that children who receive early autism therapy show significant improvements in communication, social skills, and adaptive behavior. At our neurodiverse support center in Tamil Nadu, we have seen children make remarkable progress when families take early action.
